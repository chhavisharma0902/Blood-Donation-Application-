What is this all about?

We solve the problem of blood emergencies by connecting blood donors directly with people in blood need.

What we do?

We connect blood donors with recipients, without intermediaries such as blood banks, for an efficient and seamless process.

Innovative

Blood Buddy is an innovative approach to address global health. We provide immediate access to blood donors.

Network

Blood Buddy is part of several community organizations working together as a network that responds to emergencies efficiently.

Get notified

Blood Buddy works with partners to connect blood donors and recipients through automated SMS and a mobile app.

Totally Free

Blood Buddy's ultimate goal is to provide an easy-to-use, fast, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, totally Free of cost.

Save Life

We are a non-profit foundation with the main objective of protecting vulnerable persons. Help us by making a gift!

Our Super Heroes

We depend on volunteers! Volunteers make up 96% of our workforce and carry on our humanitarian work. Blood donation is healthy, our volunteers are available 24/7 to help and donate blood. Unlike blood banks that just store blood, our volunteers respond in real-time emergencies.
